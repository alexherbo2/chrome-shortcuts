Become a sponsor to taupiqueur

Become a sponsor to taupiqueur at https://github.com/sponsors/taupiqueur. Once logged in, select one of the available tiers or choose a custom amount. If you are new to GitHub, you will need to create an account.

Amount must be at least $1. One-time sponsorships are active for 30 days.

  • Sign in with GitHub to verify your sponsorship

Sign in with GitHub to verify your sponsorship

  1. Navigate to https://github.com/login/device and choose the account you want to be signed in as.
  2. Enter your one-time code: 0000-0000.
  3. Click “Continue”. GitHub should display a page that prompts you to authorize “taupiqueur’s app” to verify your GitHub identity and know which resources you can access.
  4. Click “Authorize taupiqueur’s app”. After this, Shortcuts should be able to verify your sponsorship.
